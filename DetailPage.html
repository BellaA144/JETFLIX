ALICE IN BORDERLAND

Thriller

Drama

An aimless gamer and his two friends are stranded in parallel Tokyo. There, they are forced to compete in a series of sadistic games in order to survive.

CAST : Kento Yamazaki, Tao Tsuchiya, Nijiro Murakami

Episode 1

Arisu and his friends found a public bathroom to hide from the police. But as they exit, the streets of Tokyo are suddenly completely empty.

Episode 2

Leaving behind the injured Chota, Arisu and Karube head out to explore. When they arrive at a spacious apartment, a dangerous game of chase awaits them.

Episode 3

Chota and Shibuki's visas are about to expire, and the four enter a sprawling botanical garden in Shinjuku. There, they participate in a cruel game of betrayal.

Episode 4

Arisu is stricken with guilt and is on the verge of giving up, but Usagi encourages her to keep fighting. Next up is an endurance game on an underground highway.

Episode 5

After Arisu and Usagi make it to the Coast, the ruler of this utopian haven forces them to help collect the remaining playing cards.

Episode 6

Chishiya enlists Arisu and Usagi to help her change the status quo. An unexpected event shakes the dynamics of Pantai's fragile power.

Episode 7

Originally a shelter, Beach is now the arena for its next game: a fierce witch hunt, where everyone fights each other in vicious battles.

Episode 8

The tension in the resos reached its peak. With game time running out, those who survive now face an uncertain future.
